Rock-Paper-Scissors-GO!

    The rules are easier than if Dr. Sheldon Cooper were explaining them.
  1. Choose the picture to indiate your choice of either Rock, Paper, or Scissors
  2. Then press the 'Go' button to start the game
  3. It will be you vs the computer
  4. Play as many times as you want to see how many wins you will get
  5. Press the 'Reset' button to completely restart the game
  6. Live Long and Prosper!
